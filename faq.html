Frequently Asked Questions




This section contains a wealth of information of On The Road. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.

General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at On the Road is only required if you will be accessing personal data and using credit card to purchase. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I submit my own diary or blog?

A lot of the content of the site has been submitted by the community. You can submit your own personal diary or blog just as other people do.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for themes, templates and other items, including each seller's account balance are in U.S. Dollar
Administration

Who can publish featured recommendation?

Any registed user, who presents a work, which is genuine and appealing, can post it on On the Road. Administrator will check the content. If the blog or the diary is popular, it will automatically be pushed to the top page.

I want to publish diary - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Post section and write your own travel diary
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 2 hours.

How much do I get from each diary?

Here, at PrepBootstrap, we offer a great, 70% rate for each seller, regardless of any restrictions, such as volume, date of entry, etc.

Why do I need to login?

There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • A great 70% flat rate for your items.
  • Fast response/approval times. Many sites take weeks to process a theme or template. And if it gets rejected, there is another iteration. We have aliminated this, and made the process very fast. It only takes up to 72 hours for a template/theme to get reviewed.
  • We are not an exclusive marketplace. This means that you can sell your items on PrepBootstrap, as well as on any other marketplate, and thus increase your earning potential.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via credit card. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get refund, if I cancel my trip on the website?

We will refund you after you make a request. The refund will be posted to your account in next business day. Sometimes it also depends on your bank.
Trip

How to use plan function - what are the steps?

You can just go to the page of plan, and follow the instruction on the top to fill in your information.
Once all the information is complete, you gain full access to the final travel planner.

How to request a newsletter or a travel guidance before my trip?

You can just click on contact us button at the bottom of every page. We will contact you within half a business day.
